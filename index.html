About Us

Where emotions meet words, and stories meet people.

We provide great services and ideas

Penned Emotions was founded on 2018 by Ratnadip Nath with the sole motive to keep the art form of poetry recitation and storytelling alive.

Write-ups of diiferent people from the different corners of the Globe are taken into account and posted in the official page. We cover all genres starting right from love, life, heartbreak, inspiration, abstract, fantasy and many more.

We believe, “in a world full of temporary things you are a perpetual feeling.”

We have organized an event "AABEG",bringing out the best of the contents from budding writers.
